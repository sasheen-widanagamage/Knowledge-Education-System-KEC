Information Technology Examination

Welcome to the Information Technology Examination. Please read the following guidelines carefully before starting your exam:

  • Ensure you are in a quiet and comfortable environment free from distractions.
  • You have a total of 90 minutes to complete this exam.
  • Each question carries equal marks. Read each question carefully and provide clear, concise answers.
  • Use appropriate technical terminology where applicable.
  • Click the "Submit" button once you have completed the exam.
  • If you encounter any technical issues, please contact your instructor immediately.

Exam Questions

1. Explain the concept of Object-Oriented Programming (OOP) and its key principles.

2. Describe the differences between relational and non-relational databases.

3. What is the role of an operating system in a computer system? Provide examples.

4. Discuss the importance of cybersecurity and provide three strategies to enhance it.

5. How does cloud computing differ from traditional computing? List its advantages.

6. What is the difference between HTTP and HTTPS? Why is HTTPS preferred for secure communication?

7. Define a database and explain the difference between relational and non-relational databases.

8. Discuss the importance of network security. What are some common threats to network security?

9. What is cloud computing? Explain its main models (IaaS, PaaS, SaaS) and provide examples of each.

10. Describe the role of an operating system. How does it manage hardware and software resources?
